Who We Are

Diocesan Migrant & Refugee Services, Inc. is a non-profit organization that has assist many detained and non-detained people with low-cost services.

DMRS is one of many non-profit organizations that see many detained people and have the ability to informed them of their right by Know Your Rights presentations.

DMRS has given various services such as legal representation, assistance in documents and applications, domestic violence cases, and Know Your Rights presentations to those detained.

Our Services

We Believe that We ALL Belong

Diocesan Migrant provides various services to migrants that include: assisting with documents, know your right presentations, citizenship services, legal representation, and services for those that have been victims of domestic violence.
