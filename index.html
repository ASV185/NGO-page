Who We Are

Diocesan Migrant & Refugee Services, Inc. is a non-profit organization that has assist many detained and non-detained people with low-cost legal immigration services. DMRS has provided services to migrants since 1986, and serves Far West Texas and New Mexico.

Our Services

We Believe that We ALL Belong

Diocesan Migrant provides various services to migrants that include: assisting with documents, know your right presentations, citizenship services, legal representation, and services for those that have been victims of domestic violence.
